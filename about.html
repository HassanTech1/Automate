About us

Motivated by the successful strategic collaboration between the two companies and a shared vision for development, Idram and IDBank seamlessly consolidated under the ID Group Armenian holding.

This innovative platform seamlessly integrates banking and payment services, catering to a wide range of customer financial and transactional needs.

Today, Idram&IDBank proudly stands as Armenia's leading fintech platform, boasting over 500.000 active users and a diverse array of digital products and services in the market.

About us

Today, Idram&IDBank proudly stands as Armenia's leading fintech platform, boasting over 500.000 active users and a diverse array of digital products and services in the market.

Mission

Investing in fintech solutions to help individuals and businesses to improve their relationship with finance/transactions by providing them excellent digital solutions which are innovative, clear and simple.

Vision

ID Group envisions a world, where digital experiences redefine banking, payments and loyalty by expending the landscape of modern finance.

Values

At ID Group, our core values are innovation, customer-centricity, social responsibility and transparency.We are committed to excellence and sustainable progress.

Core Values

Client-Centric

Our customers are the heart of everything we do. We prioritize their needs, delivering personalized experience to exceed expectations.
Core Values

Innovation

We bring together finance, technology and innovation to enrich the lives of our customers by providing services that enable financial freedom.
Core Values

Transparency

We conduct business with transparency and unwavering ethical standards - it is the foundation of our relationships with clients and partners.
Core Values

Sustainability

Social responsibility is among our core values, reflecting our commitment to making a positive impact on the community and the world at large.

Shareholders

Lala Bakhshetsyan

Shareholder and general director of ID Group. Entering the fintech sector, Lala Bakhshetsyan was actively involved in the process of development of Idram company. Since 2023, she is the majority shareholder and ultimate beneficial owner of ID Group.

ID Group CJSC

Lala Bakhshetsyan is a shareholder of ID Group CJSC through DRIVELIO HOLDING LLC and MVR LLC.

Our People

Karen Nalbandian

Business Development Director of ID Group. In 2017, he joined the management team of IDBank, he is a member of the Management Board of the Bank. Has more than 20 years of international experience in the field of finance and investments.

Sergey Arakelyan

Chief Digital Officer of ID Group. Since 2018, he is also the director of digital banking of IDBank, a member of the Board. He has 9 years of experience in the financial system and more than a decade in the field of telecommunications.

Ani Harutyunyan

ID Group HR Director. Since May 2023, she has been holding the position of Group HR Director at ID Group. She has worked in various fields including banking, insurance, production, and others. Has more than 10 years of work experience in HR.

Sofi Bondarenko

Quality Assurance director at ID Group. In April 2023 joined IDBank as Head of Quality Management. In March 2024 assumed the role of Quality Assurance director at ID Group. Has over 10 years of experience in Customer Care.

Mher Abrahamyan

Chairman of the Management Board of IDBank. 2007-2012 he was the head of the banking system regulation department of the financial system regulation department of the CBA. 2012-2018 was the head of the Financial System Regulation Department. Since 2018, he is the Chairman of the Management Board of IDBank.

Anushik Khachatryan

Chief Accountant of IDBank. Since 2017, she has been the chief accountant of the Bank and a member of the Board. Has more than 24 years of working experience in the banking sector.

Arsene Kdenian

Director of Idram. 1992-2003 managed telecommunication projects of Siemens AG, 2005-2012. managed programs at Alcatel-Lucent. 2015-2017 headed the oil products quality control department of the Central Oil Laboratory of Lebanon. Since 2018, he is the director of Idram.

Mariam Hakobyan

Financial director of Idram. worked at 'Armeconombank' OJSC, Global Credit, Gladzor UCCs as chief accountant. 2012-2015 she held the position of financial director of 'SAMAN' LLC, and since 2015 she is the chief accountant/financial director of Idram.

Levon Mirzoyan

CEO of tomsarkgh.am. Founded tomsarkgh.am in 2013 and has been the CEO since then. Has over 20 years of experience in IT and e-commerce management.
